GENERAL GUIDELINES

  1. The events will be conducted only if there are a minimum of 5 teams per event
  2. All the participants are required to bring their valid College ID Cards.
  3. The fest is open to all the Computer Science and Computer Application students.
  4. The registration counter shall be open from 8:30 am to 9:30 am. Participants are requested to report at the registration desk on time.
  5. On-the-Spot registrations are allowed.
  6. Registration fee is non refundable.
  7. No accommodation will be provided.
  8. Lunch will be provided on both the days.
  9. MSRIT is not responsible for the loss of any personal belongings during the fest.
  10. Overall championship will be awarded to the college that wins the maximum number of points.
  11. Team will refrain from smoking, drugs, alcohol consumption and misbehavior. Students indulging in such unacceptable behavior shall be disqualified.
  12. The decision of the jury will be final and binding on the participants.
  13. The organizers have the right to reschedule or cancel any event(s), if the situation warrants.
